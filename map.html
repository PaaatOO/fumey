Select a Pollutant above to visualise its current levels.

Each pollutant's levels will be colour-coded at each sensor point. A key below will show the levels. Please note that All (Raw Data) is not colour coded, and includes NO2 and NO separately, as opposed to being combined as NOx.

You can see each sensor's pollutant level by clicking on it.

KEY
NO2 and NOx: Over 100ppb, 45-100ppb, 0-45ppb
CO: Over 8.7ppm, 3-8.7ppm, 0-3ppm

 Selecting "Plot only red" will display only the most dangerous levels. You could use this feature for example if you are cycling though the city and wish to travel avoiding any dangerous areas of pollution.
